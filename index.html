I am a postdoctoral researcher at Meta AI Research working with Prof. Abhinav Gupta.

My research focuses on reinforcement learning (RL), especially on exploration, transfer learning, representation learning, and multi-objective RL.

I received my PhD in Computer Science from the Technical University of Darmstadt working with Prof. Jan Peters. Before that, I recieved my MSc degree in Compute Science Engineering from the Polytechnic University of Milan under the supervision of Prof. Marcello Restelli.
